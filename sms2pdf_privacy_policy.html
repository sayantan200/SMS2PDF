Privacy Policy for Sms2Pdf

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sms2Pdf ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Sms2Pdf mobile application.

1. App Purpose

Sms2Pdf helps users convert receipt, invoice, and payment links into PDF files and save them to their Google Drive. The app may also let users import receipt-related text, links, images, or forwarded SMS content for PDF generation and organization.

2. Information We Collect or Process

Depending on the features you use, the app may process the following information:

  • Account information: name, email address, profile photo, and user ID when you sign in using Google, Apple, or Firebase Authentication.
  • Receipt and SMS-related content: SMS text, sender information, timestamps, receipt links, invoice links, and detected URLs that you manually share, paste, import, or process through the app.
  • Images and files: photos or receipt images you select from your device or capture with the camera for conversion into PDF files.
  • Generated PDFs: PDF files created from receipt links, SMS content, emails, or images.
  • Subscription information: purchase status, product ID, entitlement status, subscription expiration date, and related billing metadata handled through Google Play and RevenueCat.
  • Local app preferences: selected language, permission status, disclaimer acceptance, saved settings, queued receipt items, and app configuration values.
  • Referral information: referral codes, referral usage, share count, and related account metadata if you use referral features.

3. SMS Data

The current Android implementation does not request direct Android SMS inbox permissions such as READ_SMS or RECEIVE_SMS. Sms2Pdf processes SMS or receipt content only when you manually share, forward, paste, import, or otherwise provide that content to the app.

If SMS-related content is provided to the app, it may be used to detect receipt or invoice links, generate PDFs, save items locally, upload generated PDFs to your Google Drive, or store relevant receipt metadata in Firebase for app functionality.

4. Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access: required for sign-in, Firebase services, subscription verification, PDF generation, and cloud features.
  • Camera: used when you choose to capture receipt or document images.
  • Photos / media access: used when you choose images from your device to convert into PDFs.
  • Storage access: used to create, save, read, or manage PDF files and receipt files on supported Android versions.
  • Billing: used for Google Play subscriptions or in-app purchases.

5. How We Use Information

We use information only to provide and improve the app's functionality, including:

  • Signing users in and managing user accounts.
  • Detecting receipt, invoice, payment, and transaction links.
  • Generating PDF files from receipt links, messages, emails, or images.
  • Saving generated PDFs to your Google Drive when you choose to use Drive integration.
  • Managing subscriptions, trials, entitlements, and purchase access.
  • Providing referral and sharing features.
  • Saving local preferences and app settings.
  • Maintaining security, preventing abuse, and debugging app issues.

6. Google Drive and Google API Use

When you sign in with Google and allow Drive access, Sms2Pdf uses Google Drive permissions to create an app folder named Sms2Pdf and save generated PDF files to your Google Drive. The app uses Google access tokens only for the requested Drive operation.

Some PDF generation may be performed by a Firebase Cloud Function. In that case, the receipt URL and Google access token may be sent to the cloud function so it can generate a PDF and save it to your Google Drive. Temporary files are used for PDF generation and are deleted after the operation completes.

Sms2Pdf's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services to provide core functionality:

  • Firebase Authentication: user sign-in and account identity.
  • Cloud Firestore: user records, subscription metadata, referral data, and app data.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: server-side PDF generation and Google Drive upload support.
  • Google Sign-In and Google Drive API: account login and saving PDFs to Google Drive.
  • Google Play Billing: in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • RevenueCat: subscription status, entitlement management, and purchase syncing.

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

8. Payments

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Google Play and RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your credit card number or full payment details. We may receive purchase status, product identifiers, entitlement status, expiration dates, and related subscription metadata.

9. Data Storage and Retention

Some data is stored locally on your device, such as preferences, recently shared receipt text, generated file references, and app settings. Some account, subscription, referral, and receipt metadata may be stored in Firebase.

PDFs saved to Google Drive are stored in your own Google Drive account and remain under your control. You can delete those files directly from Google Drive.

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the app features, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our terms.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only when needed to provide app functionality, such as with Firebase, Google, RevenueCat, Google Play Billing, or other service providers used by the app.

If you choose to send or forward information through email, Gmail, SMTP, sharing, or Google Drive, that information will be handled by the relevant provider and recipient.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Choose not to grant optional permissions such as camera or photo access.
  • Delete generated PDFs from your device or Google Drive.
  • Clear app data from your device settings.
  • Sign out of the app.
  • Request deletion of account-related data by contacting us.

13. Children's Privacy

Sms2Pdf is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

14. Links to Other Websites

Receipt or invoice links processed by the app may open third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of third-party websites.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
